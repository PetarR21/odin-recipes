Pizza

Description

Pizza on the grill is a fantastic way to make pizza at home. The heat of a hot grill is a perfect match for a professional pizza oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Make the garlic oil, then brush it onto the grill grates.
  3. Shape the dough and place it on the hot grill.
